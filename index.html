Intro

I started this journey last year and since then I have been obsessed. I graduated from the Flatiron School Software Engineering program and have developed many projects and applications that have been invaluable learning experiences, each one feeding a curiosity and hunger for more. There is a link to my Github in Contact. If you are in the field, please take a look at my work. If you are aware of any current opportunities in the Austin area I would love to learn more.

So why software?

Many positions that I have had that in the past have been jobs that one could describe as "not for everybody". Sales and marketing is a good example. Some people do well and some are not really going to be a good fit and will always be envious of those who seem to fill sales positions well. I really enjoy learning new programming skills and languages and it is work that I fit into extremely well. Programming takes a level focus and self-motivation that many do not have. This is why I am making a career change. I have finally found something that I love doing and something that my personality provides a natural advantage for.
