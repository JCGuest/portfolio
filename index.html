Intro

Full-Stack and experienced Web Developer. Within two years of deciding to leave the Grocery industry, I learned the Software Engineering fundamentals, finished Boot-camp, and made a career change in favor something I am more personally passionate about. Working with decentralized platforms and Web 3.0 applications has rejuvenated and strengthened my love of Web Development. Hundreds of hours of research caused me to realize that I am in the perfect position to pivot towards this new thriving sector of Technology. I was able to realize my dream of working as a Web Developer within two years of courses and self-teaching and I look forward to what is possible for the next two years within this new exciting landscape.

I have always had a curiosity about the world around me. As a child I would pester my parents with endless questions as mundane as “how does the dishwasher work?” or “how old is the universe?”. As an adult, I have discovered a love of software. I thoroughly enjoy the process of building and coming to understand the software and technology that make up the digital world around us. Working to build the internet and use the various technologies that it encompasses is an incredibly rewarding endeavor and one that I feel fortunate to be even a small apart of. I look forward to the errors, failed tests, and debugging that comes along with any project. I find that it is only when things don't work out exactly as I expected that I learn anything new. The opportunity to learn and engineer software as a career is what motivates me the most and this journey continues to satisfy my curiosity by giving me the tools to enhance my own abilities to learn and develop new skills.

"Armed with preparation and armed with determination we pass these obstacles." - Ronald Mcnaire, physicist and astronaut.
